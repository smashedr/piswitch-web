PiSwitch

What is This?

A hardware device to automagically enter Mario Maker 2 level ID's into the Nintendo Switch in user submission situations; such as Twitch Viewer Levels.

Why do This?

Nintendo has yet to release an API or website for Mario Maker 2 and without a hardware device there is no way to programmatically enter level ID's.

When is This?

The device is in beta testing and should be available soon.
